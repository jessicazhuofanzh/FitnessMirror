BACKGROUND
It happens to most people who want to go to the gym but can’t persist. There are two reasons why these people can’t persist, laziness and busyness. There are also some reasons why they can’t stick to it because they don’t like the atmosphere of the gym, or they don’t want to work out in a crowded place, or they are not familiar with the gym equipment
PROBLEM
STATEMENT
How might we help lazy and busy people who can't keep up with fitness to be motivated and and keep up with exercise.
SOLUTION
01. Easy Onboarding

Fitmirror designed for your needs

By swiping images and introductions of Fitmirror, get excited of exercising.
02. Sign up/Log in

Provide basic information and get started

Fitmirror requires basic information like gender, date of birth, weight and height to get started. Don't forget to select your goal!
03. Features

Smart plan help you keep track of exercise

Fitmirror will generate a personalized plan for everyone. Enjoy both exercise plan and meal plan.
PAIN POINTS

  • TOO BUSY

    People sometimes are too busy at work and have no time to go to the gym even they want to​

  • BAD CONDITION

    People sometimes don't want to go the gym because of bad weather​

  • NEWBIE

    Most people are not bodybuilder, they don't know how to use many equipment, also they don't need much exercise equipment
